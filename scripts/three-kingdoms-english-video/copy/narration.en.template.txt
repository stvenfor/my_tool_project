The emperor lived in broken Luoyang.
There was not enough food in the city.
Cao Cao wanted to help the emperor.
Dong Zhao gave him a good plan.
Move the court to Xuchang, he said.
Xuchang is close and has grain.
The emperor agreed to go.
They left on the long road.
Soldiers guarded the royal carriage.
At last they reached Xuchang.
Xuchang became the new capital.
Cao Cao's power grew from here.
