#句型英文句子中文播放
1陈述句Researchers verify claims against empirical evidence.研究者依据实证证据核实主张。
2否定句The correlation did not validate the causal story.相关并未验证因果叙事。
3一般疑问句Can a small sample support a strong criterion?小样本能否支撑强标准？
4特殊疑问句Which variables confound the observed effect?哪些变量混淆了观测效应？
5选择疑问句Is the source primary evidence or secondary citation?来源是一手证据还是二手引用？
6反意疑问句The finding replicates, doesn't it?发现可复现，对吧？
7祈使句Cite original studies before generalizing widely.广泛概括前先引用原始研究。
8感叹句What careful selection criteria this trial used!这项试验用了多么仔细的选拔标准！
9存在句There is insufficient evidence for firm conclusions.没有足够证据支持坚定结论。
10被动语态Data were validated through independent audits.数据经独立审计验证。
11现在完成时Labs have refined measurement protocols over years.实验室多年来精炼测量规程。
12过去完成时Analysts had checked outliers before modeling.建模前分析师已检查异常值。
13将来时态The journal will require open data next year.该期刊明年将要求开放数据。
14真实条件句If bias enters sampling, estimates will skew.如果抽样出现偏差，估计将偏斜。
15虚拟语气If criteria were pre-registered, fishing would decline.若标准预注册，数据挖掘会减少。
16定语从句Studies that ignore confounders mislead policy.忽视混杂因素的研究误导政策。
17宾语从句Statisticians warn that significance is not importance.统计学家警告显著性不等于重要性。
18原因状语从句Because noise is high, replication is essential.因为噪声高，复现至关重要。
19让步状语从句Although trends look clear, uncertainty bands remain.尽管趋势看似清晰，不确定区间仍在。
20目的状语从句Predefine outcomes so that analysis stays honest.预先界定结果，以便分析保持诚实。
21比较结构Verified claims travel farther than rumors eventually.经核实的主张最终比谣言传得更远。
22强调句It is transparent methods that earn scientific trust.正是透明方法赢得科学信任。
23倒装句Only after reanalysis did errors become obvious.再分析之后错误才变得明显。
24分词结构Facing mixed results, the team revised hypotheses.面对混合结果，团队修订假设。
25不定式结构To verify quickly, triangulate independent sources.要快速核实，应三角验证独立来源。
26动名词结构Cherry-picking evidence undermines credibility.挑选证据会损害可信度。
27with 复合结构With larger samples, confidence intervals shrink.样本更大时，置信区间缩小。
28同位语从句The myth that one study settles debate is false.一项研究终结辩论这一迷思是错误的。
29插入评注In research, caution complements curiosity.研究中，谨慎与好奇互补。
