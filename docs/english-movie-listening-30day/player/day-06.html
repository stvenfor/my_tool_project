#句型英文句子中文播放
1陈述句Coastal cities remain vulnerable to extreme weather.沿海城市仍易受极端天气影响。
2否定句The agency did not mitigate the hazard in time.该机构未能及时缓解危害。
3一般疑问句Can resilience be built before the next crisis?能否在下一场危机前建立韧性？
4特殊疑问句What obstacles prevent effective emergency response?哪些障碍妨碍有效应急响应？
5选择疑问句Is the threat natural, human-made, or both?威胁是自然的、人为的，还是两者皆有？
6反意疑问句The system is fragile, isn't it?系统很脆弱，对吧？
7祈使句Take precautions before entering hazardous zones.进入危险区域前请采取预防措施。
8感叹句What a catastrophic failure of risk management!多么灾难性的风险管理失败！
9存在句There are early warning signs before most collapses.大多数崩溃前都有预警信号。
10被动语态Critical infrastructure must be safeguarded continuously.关键基础设施必须持续得到保障。
11现在完成时Communities have faced repeated floods this decade.本十年社区已多次遭遇洪水。
12过去完成时Officials had underestimated the scale of the disaster.官员曾低估灾害规模。
13将来时态New protocols will address bottlenecks in supply.新规程将解决供应瓶颈。
14真实条件句If tensions escalate, markets will panic quickly.如果紧张加剧，市场将迅速恐慌。
15虚拟语气If safeguards were stronger, casualties would decrease.若保障更强，伤亡会减少。
16定语从句Policies that exacerbate inequality deepen social risk.加剧不平等的政策会加深社会风险。
17宾语从句Experts warn that complacency invites future crises.专家警告自满会招致未来危机。
18原因状语从句Because loopholes remain, fraud continues to spread.因为漏洞仍在，欺诈继续蔓延。
19让步状语从句Although the conflict eased, underlying friction persists.尽管冲突缓和，深层摩擦仍在。
20目的状语从句Train responders so that emergencies do not turn chaotic.培训应急人员，以免紧急情况陷入混乱。
21比较结构Prevention is cheaper than post-disaster recovery.预防比灾后恢复更便宜。
22强调句It is weak governance that amplifies every shock.正是薄弱治理放大每一次冲击。
23倒装句Nowhere is vulnerability more visible than in slums.脆弱性在贫民窟最为可见。
24分词结构Confronting the problem early, leaders limited damage.及早正视问题，领导层限制了损害。
25不定式结构To tackle root causes, fund long-term resilience.要解决根源，应为长期韧性提供资金。
26动名词结构Ignoring warnings often leads to irreversible harm.忽视警告常导致不可逆伤害。
27with 复合结构With limited hospitals, casualties rose sharply.医院有限时，伤亡急剧上升。
28同位语从句The fear that panic itself worsens outcomes is real.恐慌本身会恶化结果的担忧是真实的。
