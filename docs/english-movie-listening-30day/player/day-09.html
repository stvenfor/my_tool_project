7祈使句Diversify investments before the bubble bursts.在泡沫破裂前分散投资。
8感叹句What a dramatic collapse in commodity prices!大宗商品价格多么剧烈的崩盘！
9存在句There is little liquidity in distressed markets.困境市场中几乎没有流动性。
10被动语态Tariffs were imposed to protect domestic industries.征收关税以保护国内产业。
11现在完成时Central banks have tightened monetary policy sharply.央行已大幅收紧货币政策。
12过去完成时Investors had fled risk assets before the crash.崩盘前投资者已逃离风险资产。

15虚拟语气If debt were sustainable, panic would be unnecessary.若债务可持续，恐慌本无必要。
16定语从句Firms that raise productivity can absorb higher costs.提高生产率的企业能消化更高成本。
17宾语从句Markets expect that unemployment will edge upward.市场预期失业率将小幅上升。
18原因状语从句Because credit tightened, small businesses struggled.因为信贷收紧，小企业陷入困境。
19让步状语从句Although exports recovered, the deficit persisted.尽管出口复苏，逆差仍在。
20目的状语从句Build reserves so that shocks do not force austerity.建立储备，以免冲击迫使紧缩。
21比较结构Capital investment is riskier than cash holdings.资本投资比持有现金风险更高。
22强调句It is consumer spending that drives short-term growth.正是消费支出驱动短期增长。

25不定式结构To restore solvency, cut nonessential expenditure.为恢复偿付能力，削减非必要开支。
26动名词结构Speculating on leverage can amplify losses rapidly.杠杆投机会迅速放大亏损。
27with 复合结构With inflation elevated, real incomes decline.通胀高企时，实际收入下降。
